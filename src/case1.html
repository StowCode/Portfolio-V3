UI/UX Case Study

Intro

This UI/UX case study focuses on a local business, Tea Cozy, to optimize the layout, branding, and accessibility of essential information. Nothing turns away a customer faster than a frustrating website! Clean and effective websites are the core of small business sucess in 2022.

Goals

  • Create interactivity for the consumer.
  • Modernize the website feel to bring in new customers. The original design feels old and aggressive.
  • Add a footer and fixed Navbar so that crucial info is always accessible.
  • Create stronger brand/culture recognition through logo placement and an appropriate color palette. The dark features of the original site, and inconsistent graphics, did not give the consumer a clear vision of the company.

Design/Mockup

Using Figma, several mockups were created that utilized the old information, and provided opportunities for interaction and growth in the future.

Click the image to view on Figma!

Conclusion / Final Design

This site now provides a better experience for consumers, and more importantly has the framework needed to add more features in the future.

Suggested additions might include integrated Yelp reviews, and a quiz that matches you with a suggested blend of tea!
